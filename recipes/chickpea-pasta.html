Chickpea Pasta

This recipe is a great replacement for your traditional pasta and is a much better alternative. No need to add protein becuase the chickpeas are a plentiful source of plant-based protein.

For the Chickpeas

  • 2 cans of Chickpeas
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1.5 tablespoons of Chili Powder
  • 1 tablespoon of Italian Seasoning
  • 0.5 tablespoons of Paprika
  • 0.5 tablespoons of Creole Seasoning (with salt)

For the Mushrooms

  • 1 package of Baby Bella Mushrooms, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of Diced Garic
  • 2 tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 tablespoon of Italian Seasoning
  • 0.5 teaspoon of chili pepper flakes

Steps

  1. First, toast the chickpeas with the spices. While this is going cook your mushrooms, garlic, and spices together to add later.
  2. Once the chickpeas are toasted and the mushrooms are finished, add the mushrooms, pasta sauce, bell peppers, and onions.
