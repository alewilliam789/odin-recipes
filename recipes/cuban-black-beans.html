Cuban Black Beans

Cuban food and seasoning is a highly underrated delicacy. In this recipe we will recreate the flavor profile of cuban black beans to provide another flavorful plant-based protein to people trying to cut meat out of their diet.

For the Black Beans

  • 2 cans of Black Beans
  • 1 can of Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon of Diced Garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of Canola Oil (or nuetral oil)
  • 1 cup of Vegetable Broth
  • 1.5 tablespoons of Chili Powder
  • 1 tablespoons of Cumin
  • 1 packet of Saizon Seasoning
  • 0.5 tablespoon of Italian Seasoning

For the Rice

  • 1.5 cups of Forbidden Rice (or high-fiber alternative)
  • 1 pointer finger......

Steps

  1. We need to use that pointer finger to cook now! Pour the rice into a rice cooker. Now place your fingerip on the bottom of the bowl and fill up to your first knuckle. Hehe gotcha! Rice cooker will do the rest.
  2. In a separate pot cook the onions, garlic, and bell peppers in the oil with saizon, chili powder, cumin, and italian seasoning. Once the onions are translucent add the beans,tomatoes, and vegetable Broth and reduce. Serve up with rice and platanos if you have the time!
